KAID

Anshul Kaushal

What happens when your own thoughts become a prison?

Anshul's debut album is a 12-track descent into a mind at war with itself. It's a raw, lyrical journey through the wreckage of past love, the paralysis of self-doubt, and the cages we build from our own memories.

Using a sharp blend of Punjabi poetry and modern grit, the album tells a single, unfolding story that culminates in a psychological civil war, where his own past confessions are weaponized by his harshest inner critic.

This isn't just an album; it's a khoj—a search. A desperate attempt to find love, not from the world, but from the one voice that matters most: the enemy within.
